Stress Testing Scenario Notes

Three scenarios are considered:
1. Baseline
2. Adverse
3. Severely Adverse

Severely adverse scenario includes:
- Unemployment increase by 3-5%
- GDP contraction
- Decline in asset prices

Impact:
- PD increases significantly under stress
- LGD may increase due to declining collateral values
- ECL rises sharply in high-risk segments such as SME and Retail
