PD Model Assumptions

The Probability of Default (PD) model is built using logistic regression and macroeconomic variables.

Key assumptions:
- PD increases with rising unemployment
- PD decreases with GDP growth
- Borrower credit score is a significant predictor
- Industry-specific risk factors influence default rates

Limitations:
- Model assumes linear relationships between macro variables and default risk
- Model performance may degrade under extreme stress scenarios
